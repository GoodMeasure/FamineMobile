this is the collective set of proficiencies and world 
knowledge that is passed down through all reincarnations.

Proficencies have diminishing returns and no cap, 
they may include:
 - Permanent Stat and weapon type boosts 
 - Bonus starting skill in various professions
 - Bonus starting Renown equal half of their ancestor's
   Renown rounded up.

World Knowledge permanently gained may include:
 - Knowledge of the class tree and talent trees
 - plant, animal, race, city, and class lore
 - Ability to select starting city in phase 0
 - Ability to select missions in certain zones
