The Six Stats

The creatures of Famine are each defined by 6 main stats that determine
their combat capabilities. They are divided into three categories:

Physical Prowess - abilities of the body & hands
 - Strength - power in leverage and size
 - Dexterity - coordination and precision of movements

Mental Capacity - abilities of the brain and mind
 - Intellect - assorted information known, cognition and memory.
 - Wisdom - general information known, understanding of universe

Spiritual Wellbeing - abilities and health of the heart and spirit
 - Faith - belief and devotion to one other than themselves, a god or leader
 - Vigor - belief in ones' self and commitment to act of free will
